Frequently Asked Questions

Can DeadlockMaster AI track my Deadlock rank and stats?
Yes. Go to the Stats tab and enter your Steam64 ID, Steam32 ID, or Steam vanity name to see your rank tier, win rate, KDA, match history, and per-hero breakdown. You can also search any other player to compare ranks.
How do I find my Steam ID?
Open your Steam profile in a browser. The URL shows your vanity name or a 17-digit Steam64 ID. You can also use steamidfinder.com to look up your ID.
Do patch notes update automatically?
Yes, fully automated. Patch notes are fetched from Steam's official news API every hour. A red dot appears on the Patches tab when a new patch drops that you have not yet read. No manual input is ever required.
Can I check my friends' stats and ranks?
Yes. Just enter their Steam ID or Steam name in the Stats tab search. Your five most recent searches are saved as quick-tap chips at the top of the tab.
Does it collect my personal data?
No. Stats are fetched using only the Steam ID you type — we do not store it. AI runs entirely on your device via Gemini Nano. External connections: public Deadlock community API (stats only), Steam news API (patch notes only), and ExtensionPay (subscription verification only).
Is DeadlockMaster AI affiliated with Valve?
No. This is an independent fan project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Valve Corporation. Deadlock is a trademark of Valve.
