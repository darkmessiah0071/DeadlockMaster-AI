Frequently Asked Questions

What is DeadlockMaster AI?
DeadlockMaster AI is a free Chrome extension that provides a complete in-browser guide for Valve's Deadlock. It includes hero builds with accurate soul costs, counter picks, advanced mechanics tutorials, live objective timers, and an on-device AI coach powered by Gemini Nano.
Does it collect any of my data?
No. All AI processing happens entirely on your device using Chrome's built-in Gemini Nano model. We do not collect browsing history, game data, or any personal information. The only external connection is to ExtensionPay.com for subscription verification.
Which heroes are in the free tier?
The free tier includes full guides for Abrams, Haze, and Infernus. The mechanics library and objective timers are also fully free with no restrictions.
How do I get 30 days of Pro free?
Open the extension, click ⚙️ Settings, and tap "Leave Review & Claim Reward." This opens your browser's Chrome Web Store review page for DeadlockMaster AI. After leaving a review, your 30-day Pro access is activated immediately — no payment needed.
Does the AI Coach work offline?
The hero guides, mechanics library, and timers work fully offline. The AI Coach requires Gemini Nano to be downloaded — once downloaded it also works offline. A smart fallback mode is available when AI is unavailable.
Is this affiliated with Valve?
No. DeadlockMaster AI is an independent fan project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Valve Corporation. Deadlock is a trademark of Valve Corporation.
